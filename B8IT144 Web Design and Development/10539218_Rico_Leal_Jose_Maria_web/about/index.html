Who are we??

Gillian Moore is a cozy and exceptional Bed & Breakfast located on the west coast of Ireland in Galway city. Come and visit us you will get nice and clean sheets, warm water and delicious food, you will come back over and over!!


Mission & Vision:

At Gillian Moore Bed & Breakfast we take care of our clients as well as family or friends and we do it this way because we feel that way, because traveling and getting to know a city also implies knowing who lives in it and we are one

of the most important parts that make your experience in Galway is as authentic as possible. This is your home. We want to contribute to improving the quality of life of our clients
